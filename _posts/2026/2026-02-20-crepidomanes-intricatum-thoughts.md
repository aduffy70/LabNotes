Thoughts and notes about this project after looking at my _Crepidomanes intricatum_ RADseq presentation from 2017:
  * I have a plot showing frequency of most common reads that I interpreted as suggesting diploidy because of the peak at 0.5 but that peak is actually between 0.5 and 0.67 so it is ambiguous for diploidy or triploidy. I suspected that it was slightly higher than 0.5 due to sequencing and assembly errors. I now have known diploid _Sphagnum_ gametophytes. Can I generate the same plot using data from those to see where the second peak falls? Does it also fall above 0.5 or does it fall at 0.5 suggesting that the higher position in _Crepidomanes_ is biologically meaningful?
  * Does cleaning vs not cleaning the _Sphagnum_ data shift that peak? I have better cleaning tools available for _Sphagnum_ but can I incorporate parts of that cleaning process into my _Crepidomanes_ data?
  * Do the _Vandenboschia_ or _Didymoglossum_ sporophytes or gametophytes look different on those plots that the _Crepidomanes_ gametophytes?
  * Are there individual samples that appear haploid in those plots? Rather than pooling everything, consider breaking it up to look at subgroups of the samples.
  * I had a plot showing inferred species based off of collector ID, rbcL RADseq markers, and atpB-rbcL PCR fragment length. How do those inferences compare to my latest species IDs from RADseq?
