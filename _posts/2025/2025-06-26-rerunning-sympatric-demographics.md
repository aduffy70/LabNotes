I have to rerun the four-species demographics analyses for the Sympatry project because I used unfolded SFS but that is only appropriate when the reference genome is an outgroup and mine is not.

I also want to rerun using more samples and letting easySFS down-sample for me rather than filtering the samples to maximize loci myself. Last time, I used 12 of each species in the four-species analysis (16 in the two-species) and selected samples with low missing data and a range of admixture levels to get loci present in 100%. It would be better to have larger sample sizes and more loci.

I am running once with the same 16 of each species and folded SFS so I can check the effect of folded vs unfolded SFS on the results.

I am also running more samples (81 _Sphagnum diabolicum_ and 36 _Sphagnum magniae_) with down-sampling in easy SFS and folded SFS to see if our limited sampling was affecting results.
